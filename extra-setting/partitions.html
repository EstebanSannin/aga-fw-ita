Partitions and Mounts

Mount, umount and check partitions /dev/sdxy
A mounted partition is automatically shared with samba

Partitions supported:

  • ext2, ext3
  • fat16, fat32 (no fsck available)
  • ntfs-3g (no fsck available)

To run fsck you need to umount the partition!
